Meet Rose Emmons

Rose Emmons has been working in the IT field since 2005, but her love of web design dates back to 1998. After years of systems administration, Rose decided it was time to pursue a new challenge, and a career change to front end development was made. In May she became a Spring/Summer cohort at Thinkful, and is on schedule to complete her studies early. Rose now spends her days writing HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery with future plans to learn Node.js. Currently she resides in blistering-hot Las Vegas with her one daughter, three dogs, and an obscene amount of Apple products.

Her hobbies include cooking, hiking, lousy attempts at photography, learning Italian, coffee shop hopping, haikus, and referring to herself in third person. She is an avid follower of space programs from around the world, and has been repeatedly accused of being an obnoxious Los Angeles Lakers and Dodgers fan.
